ABOUT

Our Story

Founded on the belief that travel should transform rather than simply transport, Odyssey Travels has been crafting extraordinary journeys for discerning travelers since 2014.

Beyond the Ordinary

What began as a passion project between two travel enthusiasts has evolved into a boutique agency that specializes in creating transformative experiences for those who seek more than just a vacation.

Based in New York City, we work with a carefully curated network of local partners around the world to ensure that every journey we craft offers authentic insights into the places and cultures you'll encounter.

We believe that the best travel experiences happen when you step away from the crowds and discover the soul of a destination.
